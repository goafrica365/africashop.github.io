Africa365 Sports Risk Free Bet Promotion

January 23, 2024 • by Editor

Exciting Opportunity for New Members

Africa365.com, the premier online sports betting platform, is thrilled to introduce the "Risk-Free Bet" promotion. This enticing offer is designed to captivate the hearts of new members, providing them with the excitement of sports betting without the fear of losing.

Exclusive Offer for New Members

The promotion is exclusively open to all new members of Africa365.com who have not previously placed any sports bets on the platform. Each new member can redeem the bonus once, offering a risk-free entry into the world of sports betting.

How to Qualify

To qualify for the risk-free bet, new members must place a minimum single bet amount of ₦200, and the condition for the result of the bet settlement is "all loses."

Generous Cap and Bonus Redemption

The bonus redemption is capped at ₦10,000 per new member, allowing bettors to explore various markets without the worry of substantial losses. To withdraw the bonus amount, members must turnover the bonus ten times, encouraging active engagement with the platform.

Claiming the Bonus

Upon successfully claiming the cashback bonus, it will be promptly allocated to the member's game account. Members can contact live chat support to streamline the process for a seamless user experience.

Conditions and Exclusions

This promotion is exclusively tailored for sports betting enthusiasts, excluding non-live table games and non-slot games unless explicitly stated otherwise. Certain conditions, such as draw results, both sides bet, voided or canceled games, and specific odds criteria, are excluded from the turnover requirement calculation.

Important Note

As with any promotion, Africa365 reserves the right to alter, amend, or cancel the Risk-Free Bet promotion at any time. Participants are urged to adhere to the listed terms and conditions, as well as the general promotion terms of Africa365.

Conclusion

The Risk-Free Bet promotion on Africa365 is a golden opportunity for new members to dive into the thrilling world of sports betting without the fear of significant losses. With carefully crafted terms and conditions, Africa365 ensures a fair and exciting experience for all participants. Don't miss your chance to claim the exclusive risk-free bet and elevate your sports betting journey on Africa365!
